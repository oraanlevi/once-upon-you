Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2025

Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights. By using twiceuponus.com you agree to this policy.

1. Information We Collect

When you place an order or interact with our site, we may collect:

  • Personal details: first name, last name, email address
  • Shipping information: address, city, postal code, country
  • Photos you upload to create your personalized book
  • Payment information (processed securely by Stripe — we never store card details)
  • Basic usage data: browser type, pages visited (via standard server logs)

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To process and fulfill your order
  • To send order confirmation and shipping updates by email
  • To provide customer support
  • To improve our products and website

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Your Photos

Photos you upload are used exclusively to create your personalized coloring book. They are stored securely on AWS S3 and are not shared, sold, or used for any other purpose. We may retain originals for a limited period to handle order issues, after which they are deleted.

4. Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party services to operate our business:

  • Stripe — payment processing. Stripe Privacy Policy
  • AWS S3 — secure photo storage
  • Resend — transactional email delivery
  • Railway — server hosting

Each of these services has their own privacy policies governing their use of data.

5. Cookies

We use minimal local storage (not tracking cookies) to preserve your order progress within a session. We do not use advertising or cross-site tracking cookies.

6. Your Rights (GDPR & CCPA)

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Opt out of any future marketing communications
  • Data portability (receive a copy of your data)

To exercise any of these rights, email us at twiceuponus@gmail.com.

7. Data Retention

Order data is retained for as long as needed to fulfill orders and comply with legal obligations (typically up to 3 years for records). Uploaded photos are deleted within 90 days of order completion unless you request earlier deletion.

8. Children's Privacy

Our site is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy periodically. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

Questions? Email us at twiceuponus@gmail.com.
