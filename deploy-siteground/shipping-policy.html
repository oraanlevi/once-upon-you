Shipping Policy

Last updated: May 2025

Every Twice Upon Us book is made to order, which means there's a production phase before it ships. Here's what to expect.

Production Time

Standard orders5–7 business days
Rush orders2–3 business days (add the Rush Order add-on at checkout)

Production time begins after your order is confirmed and payment is received.

Shipping Time

United States5–7 business days after shipment
Canada & UK7–14 business days after shipment
Rest of world10–20 business days after shipment

Total estimated delivery = production time + shipping time. You'll receive an estimated delivery window in your order confirmation email.

Shipping Carriers

We ship via USPS, UPS, or comparable carriers depending on your location. A tracking number will be emailed to you once your order ships.

Shipping Costs

Shipping costs are calculated at checkout based on your delivery address and the size of your order. We offer free shipping on qualifying orders — check at checkout to see if your order qualifies.

International Orders & Customs

International customers are responsible for any customs duties, import taxes, or fees charged by their country. These are not included in our prices and are beyond our control.

Please note that international shipments may experience delays due to customs processing.

Order Tracking

Once your order ships, you'll receive a tracking number by email. You can also check your order status anytime at twiceuponus.com using your order ID.

Incorrect Address

Please double-check your shipping address at checkout. We are not responsible for packages delivered to an incorrect address provided by the customer. Contact us immediately at twiceuponus@gmail.com if you spot an error — we can only update it before the order ships.

Lost or Stolen Packages

If your tracking shows delivered but you haven't received your package, please check with neighbors and your local carrier first. If it's still missing, email us within 7 days of the delivery date and we'll work with the carrier to resolve it.

Questions? Email us at twiceuponus@gmail.com.
