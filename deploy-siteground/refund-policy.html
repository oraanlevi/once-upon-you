Refund Policy

Last updated: May 2025

Because every Twice Upon Us book is custom-made to order using your photos, our refund policy reflects the personalized nature of what we create.

Custom products are non-refundable once production has begun. This is standard for all personalized goods. However, we always make it right if something goes wrong on our end.

1. No Refunds on Personalized Items

Once your order enters production (typically within 24–48 hours of payment), we are unable to offer a refund or cancellation. This applies to all physical personalized books and digital coloring books.

2. Cancellations

If you need to cancel your order, contact us at twiceuponus@gmail.com as soon as possible. We will do our best to accommodate cancellations before production begins, but we cannot guarantee it.

3. Damaged or Defective Items

If your book arrives damaged, misprinted, or defective due to an error on our part, we will:

  • Reprint and reship your order at no cost, or
  • Issue a full refund at our discretion

To report a damaged item, email us within 14 days of delivery at twiceuponus@gmail.com with your order number and a photo of the issue.

4. Digital Products

Digital coloring book downloads are non-refundable once the file has been delivered to your email or made available for download.

5. Lost Packages

If your order is lost in transit, please contact us and we will investigate with the carrier. We will reship or refund at our discretion once the loss is confirmed.

6. Incorrect Shipping Address

Please double-check your shipping address at checkout. We are not responsible for orders delivered to an incorrect address provided by the customer. Reshipping fees will apply in such cases.

Questions or concerns? Reach us at twiceuponus@gmail.com — we're happy to help.
